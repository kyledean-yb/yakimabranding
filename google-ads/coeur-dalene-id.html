What We Do

Managed by Experts Since 2005

Most business owners just don't have the time to correctly manage their Google Ads, which leads to unproductive, expensive campaigns — or turning off the ads altogether. We have been managing Google Ad campaigns since 2005, and they don't look anything like they did back then.

Coeur d'Alene's tourism economy creates a Google Ads opportunity that most North Idaho businesses haven't fully tapped. Visitors planning trips to the Lake City search for restaurants, activities, lodging, and services weeks before they arrive — and Google Ads can put your business in front of those searches at exactly the right moment. At the same time, year-round local customers are searching for services on a daily basis. Effective CDA Google Ads management requires building campaigns that address both audiences with the right messaging — urgency-driven for visitors making quick decisions, trust-focused for locals making long-term choices. YB Marketing understands both dynamics and builds campaigns that capture both.
