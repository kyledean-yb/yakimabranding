Graphic Standards

Graphic standards provide a sound, flexible structure for using logos, color, typography, and other brand language. Consistent brand application is essential — logos and brand elements must appear the same in every instance.

They must use proper color selections, proportions, contexts, tone, style, and voice. The standards are often more important than the logo itself — because brands that aren't consistent can seem messy, untrustworthy, and unlikeable.
