What We Do

What Is Search Engine Optimization?

SEO is the practice of earning high placement in Google search results — so customers find your business at the exact moment they're searching. We handle the strategy and the ongoing work.

Walla Walla occupies a special place in Washington's economy: a world-class wine destination, a college town anchored by Whitman College and Walla Walla University, and a tight-knit agricultural community with a growing population of food and wine tourists who plan their visits entirely online. Walla Walla businesses face a two-audience SEO challenge: capturing the local search traffic from year-round residents, and capturing the visitor-intent traffic from wine tourists, weekend travelers, and destination visitors who arrive having already decided what wineries, restaurants, and hotels to visit based on their Google research. YB Marketing builds SEO strategies that perform for both audiences — making Walla Walla businesses visible before, during, and after visitors arrive.

Personalization
We build keyword strategies around both local Walla Walla searches and the visitor-intent searches that wine tourists use when planning their trips — capturing customers at every stage of their journey.
Visibility
Rank higher for Walla Walla local searches and wine country destination searches so your business is visible to both residents and the thousands of visitors who research online before they arrive.
Spread the Word
We optimize your presence across Google Maps, TripAdvisor, Yelp, wine review platforms, and Washington travel directories — so your business appears everywhere visitors and locals search.
Google's Friend
Technical SEO improvements ensure your Walla Walla website loads fast, works on mobile, and sends the right signals to Google — critical for capturing mobile searches from visitors already in the area.
Increase Keywords
We grow your keyword reach across both local Walla Walla searches and broader Washington wine country terms that bring in regional and national visitors year-round.
Increase Traffic
More first-page rankings for both local and visitor searches mean steady, year-round organic traffic from the customers who matter most to your Walla Walla business.
SEO vs. PPC

Which Is Right for Your Business?

Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising offers immediate visibility and control, while SEO builds organic, sustainable traffic over time. Many businesses benefit from both. Get in touch with YB to find the right mix for your goals.

SEO
  • ✓Long-term, sustainable organic traffic
  • ✓Builds authority and trust with Google
  • ✓Compounds in value — results grow over time
  • ✓Ranks in both traditional and AI search results
PPC / Google Ads
  • ✓Immediate visibility at the top of Google
  • ✓Full control over budget and targeting
  • ✓Great for promotions, launches, and events
  • ✓Measurable ROI on every dollar spent
