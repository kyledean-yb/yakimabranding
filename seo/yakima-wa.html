SEO vs. PPC

Which Is Right for Your Business?

Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising offers immediate visibility and control, while SEO builds organic, sustainable traffic over time. Many businesses benefit from both. Get in touch with YB to find the right mix for your goals.

SEO
  • ✓Long-term, sustainable organic traffic
  • ✓Builds authority and trust with Google
  • ✓Compounds in value — results grow over time
  • ✓Ranks in both traditional and AI search results
PPC / Google Ads
  • ✓Immediate visibility at the top of Google
  • ✓Full control over budget and targeting
  • ✓Great for promotions, launches, and events
  • ✓Measurable ROI on every dollar spent
