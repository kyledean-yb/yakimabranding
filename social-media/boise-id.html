What We Do

Social Media Management & Growth

Social media is one of the most powerful tools for building brand awareness, engaging your audience, and driving real business results — but only when it's managed with a real strategy behind it.

Boise's social media landscape reflects the city's rapid growth and demographic evolution. The city's large influx of newcomers — many from coastal cities with high social media expectations — means your content quality bar is higher than it was just a few years ago. At the same time, Boise's strong sense of local identity (Treasure Valley pride, outdoor lifestyle, farm-to-table culture, Boise State sports fanaticism) creates powerful content hooks for businesses that know how to use them. YB Marketing creates Boise social content that appeals to both longtime residents and the city's growing transplant community — building the kind of authentic local brand presence that drives real engagement and customer loyalty.
